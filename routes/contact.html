Music Submissions

Are you an artist or label wanting to get your music on RDU? We accept physical and digital submissions.

Physical submissions: Send CDs or vinyl to our address above

Digital submissions: Email download links or streaming links to music@rdu.org.nz

Please include artist bio, press photos, and any relevant information about your release. We review all submissions and add music that fits our programming to our library.
