About RDU 98.5 FM

RRDU98.5FM, also known as Radio U, UFM or RDU, is a radio broadcast platform based in Ōtautahi Christchurch. We support alternative voices; musicians, students and broadcasters alike. For almost 50 years, we’ve championed the underground and challenged the mainstream.
